Features

Built for serious students

🎭

8 hero classes

Choose from Knight, Mage, Druid and more. Each hero is locked to one subject so your RPG character grows with your knowledge.

⚔️

Three difficulty modes

Easy, Normal, and Hard — each with different HP, question counts, and XP multipliers. Push yourself or ease into new material.

🃏

Flashcards + quizzes

Study with smart flashcards first, then test yourself with quiz battles. Wrong answers are reviewed at the end of each run.

Chain correct answers for combo multipliers up to 3x XP. Daily quests and achievements keep you coming back.
